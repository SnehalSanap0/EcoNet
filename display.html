EcoNet

Welcome to our educational game project, designed to teach students about Globe Protocol systems through engaging gameplay.

Cloud Quest

Players identify different types of clouds and weather conditions.

Gameplay: Match clouds to the correct types and maintain balance in the environment. Incorrect selections cause imbalances that affect weather patterns.

Educational Outcome: Teaches kids about cloud types and their role in weather.

Ecosystem Builder

Players create a balanced ecosystem.

Gameplay: Add plants, animals, and environmental elements to sustain life. Balance the needs of each organism.

Educational Outcome: Helps students understand ecosystem interactions.

Water Cycle Adventure

Navigate through the water cycle.

Gameplay: Overcome challenges like pollution and droughts while ensuring water cleanliness.

Educational Outcome: Teaches about the water cycle and its importance.

Soil Detective

Test soil quality and help plants grow.

Gameplay: Select the right soil amendments for different plants to thrive.

Educational Outcome: Teaches about soil types and health.

Earth Harmony: Connect the Spheres

Connect different Earth systems to restore balance.

Gameplay: Players link systems together to create a harmonious Earth.

Educational Outcome: Demonstrates the interconnectedness of Earth's systems.
